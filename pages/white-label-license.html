White Label Licence

A white label licence (or re-seller licence) enables you to take the system and remove all credits from the existing developer, thus making the software appear as your own. The cost of the white label licence is not cheap, but its a lot cheaper than hiring a programmer to build something from scratch. If you are good at marketing and can sell the software you`ll soon recuperate the costs and be on your way to making lots of money.

The main features are:

  • Remove all references to A&M Digital Technologies or AMDT,LLC
  • Sell the system as your own at any price on any website
  • Get a great system at a very low price. Cheaper than hiring a programmer.
  • 100% source code access

Do I get any support?

Basic support is covered by the standard licence and the 12 month priority support. You can renew the priority support after 12 months if you wish. Note that not support is offered for custom changes made to the system after the licence is purchased. If you break something you will have to fix it yourself.

When I purchase the white label license, do I purchase the software outright?

No, the software is copyrighted and remains the property of A&M Digital Technologies, LLC

How much does it cost?

See the http://giveu.amdtllc.com page for the current price.

Are they any upgrade fees for future versions?

No, the white label licence covers ALL future versions.

Can I hire to make custom changes even after I have purchased white label license?

Yes, of course. Support for such custom changes will be offered for a period of 6 months. Support includes bug fixes and security update but not additional features or modifications.

Is the white label license refundable?

Initially yes, but once you receive the files for 100% source code access, the licence is NOT refundable.

What is allowed?

The following is allowed with the white label licence:

  • Remove all our copyright references
  • Sell the system as your own at any price on any website.
  • Make modifications to suit your own requirements
  • Integrate the software with any 3rd party software
  • Set up and market the software as a subscription based system

What is not allowed?

The following is NOT allowed with the white label licence:

  • Make any reference to our company as a partner
  • Sell the system with any reference to our company name in the title
  • Offer the system for free as a direct competitor for our main product and/or our company
  • Make a copy of the our website for your own marketing purposes
  • Use any of support website material for your own marketing purposes
